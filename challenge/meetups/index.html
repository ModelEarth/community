US EPA + Code for America

View Project Areas - International participants are welcome!

Our collaboratively developed tools report the environmental and economic impact of industries in local communities and states using 24 indicators, including jobs, value-added, air quality, climate impact, energy use, water use and land use. The lifecycle tools use the US EPA’s USEEIO model for estimating environmental profiles of industries and goods and services, combined with local community data including employment, industry, and geographic data to provide community-specific insight.
